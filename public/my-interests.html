My Interests

I've got what you could call a pretty wide range of interests. I'm into cycling and running to keep fit and have fun, okay, the fun bit only really applies to cycling, and specifically, offroad cycling!

I'm also very interested in cars/motorsport and love to tinker and spend too many hours cleaning my car. I also got a motorbike last year and I'm really enjoying life on two wheels where I don't get all puffed out!

And then there's Scouting. Scouting has nearly always played a part in my life and I honestly think its the best thing I've ever done. Once I turned 18 I joined the adult section of Scouts in my local area (we call ourselves "Roosters") and since then I've met a bunch of like minded people of varying age, nearly every Thursday to do some sort of activity, followed by a visit to a local pub. There's actually so much I can say about what I've done in Scouting that I couldn't possibly document it all here, so feel free to ask me!
